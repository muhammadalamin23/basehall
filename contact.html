DIRECTOR OF SALES AND MARKETING BASEHALL
POSITION

Reporting to the MD, the successful candidate will be responsible for the continued development of BaseHall and its existing brands, alongside leading the brand development and launch of Arcade Battersea Power Station, and any future national
or international locations. The candidate will oversee Marketing, Sales and Business Development.

Responsibilities include
  • Manage conception, development, and implementation of marketing plan for Arcade(s) and associated brand(s)
  • Work closely with the brand founders to set the strategic priorities for the brand, identifying and establishing how to maximise opportunities to drive the business and create key consumer target groups by category and channel.
  • Manage sales and business development to ensure maximisation of all revenue opportunities for Arcade from general footfall and delivery to group bookings, events, and exclusive hires.
  • Develop and execute a digital marketing strategy for Arcade and associated brand which builds the brand(s) and delivers key commercial objectives.
  • Develop and execute a strategy for Arcade delivery in conjunction with Deliveroo, Reef etc
  • Driving market leading brand engagement on key social media platforms through effective content creation.
